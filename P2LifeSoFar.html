College

After High School, I was accepted into Virginia Tech with the intention of going head first into the College of Engineering and studying Computer Science. Plans changed, and I found an opportunity to pivot into the College of Science to then study Computational Modeling and Data Analytics with a minor in Computer Science!

In that time, I became the Webmaster for a Latinx organization called Latin Link, where I created a website for the organization using Google Sites! Not only that, I, along with a group of close friends revived the Latino Fraternity known as La Unidad Latina, Lambda Upsilon Lambda, Fraternity Inc. We publicized our leadership through academics, culture, brotherhood and service.

College taught me a lot. Not only was it about the classes and academic knowledge, but the major life-lessons that I reflect upon every day. College prepared me for the real world, and it prepared me to dive into the pursuits that I truly love!
